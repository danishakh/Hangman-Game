HANGMAN

This is a classic hangman game where you get a specific number of chances to guess the word(s) correctly. You will lose a chance to guess only for every incorrectly guessed letter.

There are a few different categories that you can choose from, once you have selected your category, the page will automatically update the guess word. To guess a letter, just press that letter key on your keyboard!

Should be easy enough, right? Have fun!
